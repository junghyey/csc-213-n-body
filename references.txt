
 *  Physics Foundation:                                                        *
 *  [1] Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation:                                *
 *      F = G * (m1 * m2) / r^2                                              *
 *      where G is the gravitational constant, m1,m2 are masses,              *
 *      and r is the distance between bodies                                  *
 *                                                                            *
 *  Numerical Methods:                                                         *
 *  [2] Euler Integration Method:                                             *
 *      x(t+dt) = x(t) + v(t)*dt                                             *
 *      v(t+dt) = v(t) + a(t)*dt                                             *
 *      where dt is timestep, x is position, v is velocity                    *
